Disorders

  • Panic disorder
  • Obsessive-compulsive disorder
  • Phobias
  • Depression
  • Bipolar disorder
  • Eating disorders
  • Personality disorders
  • Post-traumatic stress disorder
  • Psychotic disorders, including schizophrenia

Symptoms

  1. Feeling sad or lonely
  2. Confused or pressured thinking
  3. Having no friends
  4. Increasing mood changes
  5. Tiredness or trouble sleeping
  6. Nightmares, flashbacks, and/or hallucinations
  7. Unable to cope with daily problems, including war events, COVID-19, having moved to a new city, or even death
  8. Out-of-control violence
  9. Major changes in your diet

Treatments

Your treatment depends of what disorder you've suffered so there is a variety of them, including visiting a local treatment squad, taking medications, psychotherapy, and participating to take care of yourself.
